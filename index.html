Circle Bisection

Draw one curve that starts outside the circle, passes through it, and ends outside again. The closer the two pieces are in area, the higher your score.
✂️ One cut only
Cuts
0 / 1
You get one freehand attempt per round.
Uniformity

Make a valid cut to see your result.
How to play
  1. Start drawing from outside the circle.
  2. Cross through the circle once.
  3. End the stroke outside the circle.
